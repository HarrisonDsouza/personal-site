A little About me

Hey there! I am Harrison Daniel Dsouza, a dedicated individual with a passion for new ideas and exploring different things. Curiosity is my superpower – I’m always looking for a challenge and something new to learn. Whether it’s coding, designing a website or just figuring out how things work, I’m in. My goal is to use my curiosity and skills to create something meaningful and innovative that makes a difference. I want to combine creativity with technology to solve real world problems and grow personally and professionally.

When I’m not working on tech projects you’ll find me hanging out with family and friends, watching movies or drinking a good cup of coffee. I’m also a big music fan – there’s nothing like a good playlist to get the creative juices flowing. And pizza? Definitely my go-to! I’m always up for a new adventure and opportunity to learn and grow. So, if you have a project or just want to chat about the latest tech, music or anything else, hit me up!
